# Mood recipes for /design-mood

Each recipe sets all six levers: color, type, shape, space/pace, motion, texture — plus copy voice and a litmus question. Hex values are starting points; tune to the product, keep the relationships.

---

## calm
*For: meditation, health, journaling, reading, finance-that-reassures.*
- **Color:** low-contrast cool-to-neutral — sage `#9CAF97`, mist blue `#AEC3CE`, sand `#E8E0D4` on warm off-white `#FAF7F2`; ink soft charcoal `#33393B` (never pure black). Accents barely louder than the base.
- **Type:** humanist sans (**Hanken Grotesk**, **Public Sans**) or gentle serif (**Lora**) — light/regular weights, body 17–18px, line-height 1.7, generous measure.
- **Shape:** softly rounded (8–16px), borderless surfaces separated by tone shifts rather than lines.
- **Space/pace:** airy — one idea per viewport, section spacing 100–140px, low information density.
- **Motion:** slow fades and gentle drifts, 400–600ms ease-out; nothing enters from off-screen; no parallax.
- **Texture:** flat or faint paper warmth; soft large-radius shadows at very low opacity if any.
- **Copy:** short, present-tense, no exclamation marks. "Take a moment." not "Boost your mindfulness!"
- **Never:** red badges, counters, or anything that ticks; high-contrast CTAs shouting; springy motion.
- **Litmus:** would this page lower a heart rate?

## energetic
*For: fitness, sports, music, launches, youth brands.*
- **Color:** high contrast + heat — electric coral `#FF4D36` or lime `#C8F135` on near-black `#141414`, or clashing brights on white. Saturation high, accents collide on purpose.
- **Type:** condensed/heavy display (**Archivo Black**, **Anton**) at huge sizes, italic slants for speed; body a sturdy grotesque.
- **Shape:** sharp or angled — diagonal section cuts, skewed highlight bars, 0–6px radius.
- **Space/pace:** dense and rhythmic — alternating loud/quiet bands; numbers and stats oversized.
- **Motion:** fast (150–250ms), snappy ease-out, staggered slams; marquees; count-up numbers; hover states that punch (scale 1.05, instant color flips).
- **Texture:** flat and hard; halftone or noise on photos for grit.
- **Copy:** imperative verbs, second person, short. "Go again." "Beat yesterday."
- **Never:** pastels, gentle fades, thin light type, polite spacing everywhere.
- **Litmus:** does scrolling feel like a beat is playing?

## trustworthy
*For: banking, insurance, healthcare, B2B infrastructure, government.*
- **Color:** deep cool anchor — navy `#16294A` or slate `#27303B` with disciplined white space; ONE restrained accent (steel blue `#3B6FA0` or verified green `#2E7D5B`). Functional colors muted, consistent, never decorative.
- **Type:** even, open sans (**Source Sans 3**, **IBM Plex Sans**) — regular weights, no quirks; tabular figures for every number; clear 1.25-ratio hierarchy with no jumps.
- **Shape:** modest radius (4–8px), real borders (1px, visible) — edges and structure signal accountability.
- **Space/pace:** ordered and symmetric — aligned label/value pairs, tables that look like well-kept ledgers, nothing floating or overlapping.
- **Motion:** minimal and functional — 150–200ms state confirmations only; a check that draws once on success. Nothing playful.
- **Texture:** none. Flat, clean, exact.
- **Copy:** plain, complete sentences, numbers with units, no hype. "Your transfer arrives Tuesday, June 16." Disclose fees before being asked.
- **Never:** gradients, countdown timers, dark patterns, marketing adjectives near money, cartoon illustrations in serious flows.
- **Litmus:** would you type your salary into this page?

## nostalgic
*For: heritage brands, photo/memory products, music archives, anniversaries.*
- **Color:** faded warmth — mustard `#D9A441`, burnt orange `#C26A38`, avocado `#7A8450`, cream `#F3EAD3`, brown ink `#3E2F23`. Everything slightly desaturated, as if sun-aged.
- **Type:** era-true faces — slab/serif warmth (**Recoleta**-like: try **Gambetta**; or **Cooper**-adjacent heavy rounded serifs) for display; clean retro-grotesque or typewriter mono for support.
- **Shape:** rounded-rectangular like old TV screens and ticket stubs; scalloped or stamped edges as accents.
- **Space/pace:** album-like — framed photos with thick borders and captions, generous mats, contents arranged like a scrapbook spread (slight rotations, taped corners — used sparingly).
- **Motion:** gentle crossfades like a slide carousel (400ms); a subtle film-grain shimmer at most; no modern springs.
- **Texture:** the signature lever — paper grain, photo borders, halftone dots, slight vignette on imagery (2–6% opacity overlays; suggestion, not Instagram filter).
- **Copy:** storytelling past tense, specific years and places. "Since 1974." "The summer it all started."
- **Never:** neon, glassmorphism, sharp flat-design crispness, system-default type.
- **Litmus:** does it feel found rather than made?

## futuristic
*For: AI products, dev infrastructure, aerospace, crypto-adjacent, sci-fi media.*
- **Color:** dark-dominant — near-black blue `#0A0E14` with luminous accents: cyan `#4DEAFF`, signal green `#3DFFA0`, or white-hot `#EAF6FF`; accents GLOW (`box-shadow: 0 0 24px` at 30%). Light mode variant: paper white + one electric accent, ultra-sparse.
- **Type:** precise geometric sans (**Space Grotesk**, **Geist**) + mono (**JetBrains Mono**, **Space Mono**) for data/labels; wide tracking on uppercase micro-labels ("SYSTEM ONLINE"); thin-to-regular weights.
- **Shape:** sharp with surgical exceptions — 0–4px radius, hairline borders at 15% white, corner-tick decorations on panels (the HUD signature), clipped corners.
- **Space/pace:** instrument-panel density where data lives, void-like emptiness around heroes; layered translucent panels (true transparency + 1px line, NOT blurry glassmorphism).
- **Motion:** the believer's lever — text that types/decodes in, numbers that tick, panels that draw their borders (300ms), scan-line sweeps on load, cursor-reactive glows. Fast and precise (≤250ms), never bouncy.
- **Texture:** faint grid lines in empty space, scan-line or dot-matrix at 2–3%, glow only on interactive/live elements.
- **Copy:** terse, technical, lowercase-or-caps system voice. "initializing." "3 nodes active."
- **Never:** purple-blue SaaS gradients (the fake-future tell), chrome/lens-flare skeuomorphism, rounded friendly buttons, warm paper tones.
- **Litmus:** does it feel like an instrument you've been cleared to operate?

## organic
*For: food, skincare, sustainability, outdoors, farming, wellness brands.*
- **Color:** from the ground — moss `#5B6B4A`, terracotta `#B5654A`, clay `#D8C3A5`, bark `#4A3C2E` on unbleached `#F7F2E9`; nothing fluorescent; greens olive-leaning, never bootstrap-green.
- **Type:** humanist with warmth — serif (**Fraunces** soft axis, **Lora**) display, humanist sans body; slightly larger body (17–18px); no condensed faces.
- **Shape:** the irregular lever — blob masks on images, hand-drawn SVG underlines and arrows, asymmetric border-radius (`40% 60% 55% 45%`), wavy dividers; nothing perfectly symmetric.
- **Space/pace:** uneven, natural rhythm — elements sit at varied offsets like things growing, not a strict grid (but an underlying grid keeps it from chaos).
- **Motion:** slow sway and drift (4–8s loops, ±4–8px) on decorative elements; reveals like unfurling (clip-path from bottom, 500ms); nothing mechanical or instant.
- **Texture:** real materials — paper grain, watercolor edges, pressed-leaf illustrations, photography with natural light only.
- **Copy:** sensory and concrete. "Cold-pressed within four hours of harvest" beats "premium quality".
- **Never:** pure geometric precision, neon, dark mode by default, plastic gradients, stock photos with white backgrounds.
- **Litmus:** could any element have a fingerprint on it?

## somber
*For: memorials, serious journalism, museums of difficult history, condolence flows, incident reports.*
- **Color:** deep restraint — charcoal `#23211F`, ash `#8B8680`, bone `#EFEBE4`; at most one muted accent (dried-rose `#9B6A6A` or candle `#C9A86A`) used once. High readability, low stimulation.
- **Type:** a dignified serif (**Source Serif 4**, **EB Garamond**) — this register belongs to the serif; modest sizes, nothing huge; generous leading (1.7–1.8).
- **Shape:** none to notice — hairline rules, square or barely-rounded edges, no decorative shapes.
- **Space/pace:** silence as design — very generous spacing, one statement per view, slow vertical rhythm like a procession.
- **Motion:** near none — slow opacity (600ms+) at most; absolutely nothing bounces, slides, or delights.
- **Texture:** faint paper at most.
- **Copy:** plain, exact, unhedged, human. Names and dates correct. No euphemistic corporate softeners.
- **Never:** badges, CTAs that "pop", social-share confetti, autoplay anything, upsells anywhere near grief.
- **Litmus:** could this page sit in a quiet room without speaking?

## urgent
*For: incident dashboards, emergency info, deadline flows, alerts. (Use honestly — fake urgency is a dark pattern.)*
- **Color:** alarm grammar — red `#D92D20` and amber `#F79009` reserved EXCLUSIVELY for the urgent things, on calm dark `#16181D` or clinical white; everything non-urgent deliberately muted so the signal owns the room.
- **Type:** instantly legible — bold grotesque (**Inter** is acceptable here — legibility outranks personality), big numerals, mono for timestamps/IDs; key facts at 2–3× body size.
- **Shape:** sharp, bordered, demarcated — status banners full-width, severity badges rectangular and consistent.
- **Space/pace:** triage hierarchy — the ONE most important thing unmissable at top, everything else visibly subordinate; dense where operators need data, never decorative.
- **Motion:** only meaning-bearing — a new-alert slide-in (150ms), a pulsing live-dot (the only allowed pulse); no ambient animation competing for attention.
- **Texture:** none.
- **Copy:** front-loaded facts: what happened → impact → action, in that order. "Database failover at 14:32 UTC. Reads degraded. Mitigation running." Timestamps everywhere; relative + absolute.
- **Never:** red used decoratively elsewhere, fake countdowns, ALL CAPS paragraphs, blinking text, burying the action below the fold.
- **Litmus:** in three seconds, does a stressed person know what happened and what to do?

## dreamy
*For: creative tools, music/art platforms, kids' bedtime products, fantasy media, ambient experiences.*
- **Color:** dusk gradients done right — large, soft, multi-stop atmospheric washes (lavender `#B8A6D9` → peach `#F2C4B3` → pale sky `#BFD8E8`) as ENVIRONMENT (backgrounds), never as text fills or button paint; ink soft indigo `#2E2A4F`.
- **Type:** light and floating — an airy serif (**Cormorant**, light) or thin geometric sans, generous tracking; nothing heavy or condensed.
- **Shape:** soft everything — large radii (20px+), feathered shadows, elements that fade at their edges (radial masks), floating cards with no hard borders.
- **Space/pace:** weightless — elements at varied depths, overlapping translucent layers, slow drifting offsets; horizons low, sky generous.
- **Motion:** the defining lever — everything floats: 6–10s drift loops, parallax clouds, blurs that resolve on focus, 600–900ms dissolve transitions; slow but never laggy on input.
- **Texture:** soft grain in gradients (kills banding, adds atmosphere), bokeh dots, glow used tenderly.
- **Copy:** evocative, lowercase-friendly, unhurried. "somewhere to put the songs you half-remember."
- **Never:** hard borders, alarm colors, dense tables, snappy spring physics, corporate CTA blocks crashing the atmosphere.
- **Litmus:** does the page feel like it would make no sound if it fell?

## cozy
*For: reading apps, recipe sites, small communities, indie games, journaling, cafés.*
- **Color:** firelight — cream `#F6EEDF`, caramel `#C68B59`, rust `#A4543A`, deep brown ink `#3C2E25`; warm at every step (even shadows are warm-tinted); dark mode is candlelit brown-black `#221C16`, never blue-black.
- **Type:** a friendly serif for text (**Lora**, **Source Serif 4**) — like a well-printed paperback; rounded sans for UI; body 17–18px, comfortable 1.65 leading.
- **Shape:** soft and thick — 12–16px radius, chunky padding, buttons that look cushioned; thick (2px) warm borders on key cards like stitched edges.
- **Space/pace:** snug, not cramped — closer spacing than "calm" (a full room, comfortably arranged); content in a cozy max-width column (~680px) rather than spanning wide.
- **Motion:** small warm acknowledgments — gentle 200–300ms ease, a soft settle (tiny overshoot, far from playful-springy), page-turn or fade for transitions.
- **Texture:** strongest here — paper grain, fabric weave at 2–4%, hand-drawn dividers, steam curls and mug-ring illustration accents (one or two per view).
- **Copy:** first-person warmth, like a note from a friend. "We saved your spot on page 142."
- **Never:** clinical white, sharp corners, cold grays, urgency mechanics, infinite-scroll restlessness, anything that feels like an office.
- **Litmus:** does opening it feel like coming inside from the rain?
